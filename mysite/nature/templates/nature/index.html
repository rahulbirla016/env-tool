Help the Environment

There are many ways to help the earth. You can make a difference.


The Paris 1.5 degrees C Agreement was made by countries to help battle the effects of climate change. Countries, as part of the agreement, aim to be net-zero in their carbon footprint by the year 2050. This is to reduce the earth warming by more than 1.5 degrees Celsius. Scientists say that a 2 degrees celsius increase will lead to irreparable issues on earth, so the agreement aims to get all the countries in the world to aim for net-zero emissions to avoid these catastrophic issues.


Take small steps in your life. Each step matters.

Ways to help

  1. Plant a tree
  2. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  3. Learn more about environmental issues
  4. Spread the word!
